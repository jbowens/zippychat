Possible questions

Are there any plans for more than two hundred concurrent users?

If you expect more than 200 concurrent users and are interested in Zippy Chat, contact support. We can construct a custom plan to accommodate your traffic.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. At any time you can switch plans through your chat room control panel. You'll receive a pro-rated charge or refund based on your new plan's cost and the time remaining on your current term.

What happens if I exceed my limit of concurrent users?

The limits are soft caps, so nothing at first. If you continually exceed your limit or exceed it by a significant amount, we'll contact you asking you to switch plans to continue using Zippy Chat.

How do I cancel service?

Cancelling is easy and can be done through your chat room control panel.

Can I host a Zippy Chat chat room myself?

No. Currently, we only offer Zippy Chat chat rooms as a hosted web service.
